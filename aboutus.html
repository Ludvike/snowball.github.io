About Us

We in Save The Sealife are on a mission to stop the gradual increasing pollution that is being inflicted upon the oceans. We plan on solving this problem by making a new kind of plastic which is made in such a way that it tastes gross for all fishes eating it. And thereby hindering the fishes from eating the polluted plastic. From this point it would be easier to pick up tha plastic and pollution without hurting the animals. We believe this will play a part in saving the increasing pollution problems we face today.
